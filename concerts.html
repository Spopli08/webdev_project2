My Concert Experiences

I love attending concerts and experiencing live music! Here are my last three concert experiences that were truly unforgettable.

Concert 1: Prateek Kuhad at Thalia Hall

I've been following Prateek Kuhad since 2018, and this summer, I finally had the chance to meet him at his concert at Thalia Hall. The music was so soothing and created an incredibly memorable experience. It was amazing to finally hear him live and connect with the music I've loved for years.

Concert 2: Diljit Dosanjh at Allstate Arena, Rosemont

Diljit Dosanjh, a renowned Indian Punjabi singer and actor, has made history as the first Punjabi artist to perform at the prestigious Coachella festival. I had the opportunity to witness his electrifying performance at the Allstate Arena in Rosemont. His unique blend of traditional Punjabi music with modern beats brought an incredible energy to the show. The audience was on their feet throughout, dancing and singing along to his hits.

Concert 3: Post Malone in United Center, Chicago

Although it has been almost two years, Post Malone's concert in Chicago holds a special place for me as it was the first concert I attended after moving to the city. Known for his unique blend of hip-hop, rock, and pop, Post Malone delivered an unforgettable performance, featuring hits like Circles, Rockstar, and Sunflower.
